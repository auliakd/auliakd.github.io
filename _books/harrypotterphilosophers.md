*Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone* is the first book in the **Harry Potter** series by **J. K. Rowling**.  
It follows the story of an orphaned boy who discovers that he is a wizard and is invited to attend the Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry.

I'm currently reading this book. I love how the story feels warm and full of curiosity, with a touch of humor.  
The magical world feels alive, like a place that could exist just outside our everyday reality.
